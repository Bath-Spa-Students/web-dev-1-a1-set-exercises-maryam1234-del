Overview

"Riverdale" is a modern-day reimagining of the classic Archie Comics characters, featuring a mix of teen drama, mystery, and dark themes set in a small town filled with secrets.

Cast

  • kj Apa as Archie Andrews
  • lili teunhart as betty cooper
  • camila mendes as veronica lodge
  • cole sprouse jughead jones
  • marisol nicholas as hermoine lodge
  • madelaine petsch as Walter cheryl blossom.

Reviews
